How COVID affected my productivity

12 August 2021

TL;DR I work less but I feel more productive.

  • Context: I live in Melbourne, Australia.
  • We have had strict lockdowns for many months.
  • We have had multiple lockdowns (currently in our 6th).
  • During this time I have worked exclusive from home (except for 2 days in between lockdowns).
  • My job is a UI designer at Maths Pathway.
  • For personal reasons, I track my work hours and note down if I "felt productive". I have been doing this every work day since June 2017.

I work less now. After the transition to full time remote work, there was a noticeable decrease in my working hours.
